Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

1. Overview

Journal Your Trades ("we", "us", "our") is committed to your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Journal Your Trades at journalyourtrades.app ("the Service"). The short version: your trade data never leaves your device.

2. Data We Collect

What we do NOT collect:

  • Your trade data, portfolio, or financial information
  • Your browsing behaviour within the app
  • IP address or device fingerprints (beyond standard web logs)

What we may collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store card details.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymised, aggregated data (e.g. which features are used) via privacy-friendly analytics (no cookies, no cross-site tracking).

3. How Your Trade Data is Stored

All trade data — your entries, exits, notes, tags, strategies — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data exists only on your device. We have no access to it, cannot read it, and cannot recover it if you clear your browser data.

4. AI Features

When using the AI Coach feature: if you use your own Anthropic API key, your trade data is sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses, subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. If using Ollama (local), all processing happens on your machine — nothing is sent anywhere.

5. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for authentication (session management). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — AI features (optional). Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention

Account data (name, email) is retained until you delete your account. Trade data is never on our servers, so there is nothing to retain or delete on our end.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, export, and delete your account data at any time. Email us at privacy@journalyourtrades.app to exercise these rights.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Journal Your Trades really free? ▸
Yes — 100% free, forever, with no paid plan. No credit card, no hidden tiers, no feature gates. Every single feature is available to every user, always.
Where is my data stored? ▸
Entirely in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device. We have no servers that receive your trade data.
Which brokers are supported? ▸
Any broker that exports CSV trade history. We have a one-click CSV importer. We also support Stocks, Crypto, Forex, Futures, and Options — all in the same journal.
Can I use Journal Your Trades on mobile? ▸
Yes. Journal Your Trades is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. No app download required.
What AI model powers the AI Coach? ▸
The AI Coach is powered by Claude (Anthropic). You bring your own API key — so your data stays private and you're in control of costs.
How do I export my data? ▸
Go to Journal → Export CSV. You get a full CSV of all your trades that you can open in Excel, Google Sheets, or any other tool.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

TL;DR — Your data never leaves your device.

Journal Your Trades is a client-side application. All trade data, journal entries, goals, and settings are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your trading data in any form.

What we collect

Nothing personal. Journal Your Trades does not collect names, email addresses, trade data, financial information, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., when contacting support).

If you use the optional AI Coach feature, queries are sent directly to the Anthropic API using your own API key. Anthropic's own privacy policy governs that interaction — we do not see, log, or store your AI conversations.

Cookies & Tracking

Journal Your Trades does not use tracking cookies, analytics pixels, or third-party tracking scripts. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar services.

Local Storage

All application data is stored in your browser's localStorage under the keys tf_trades, tf_goals, tf_rules, and tf_user. This data exists only on your device. Clearing your browser data removes it permanently — we cannot recover it.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service Journal Your Trades may communicate with is the Anthropic API (for the optional AI Coach). This communication happens directly from your browser using your own API key. No other third-party services receive your data.

Data Export & Deletion

You can export all your data at any time via Journal → Export CSV. To delete all data, use Settings → Clear All Data, or clear your browser's localStorage. We have no copy of your data to delete.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email us at privacy@journalyourtrades.app
